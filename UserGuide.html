Creating a Recipe or Meal

Creating a Recipe or Meal.

In the menu bar, locate the Edit menu item.
Click on the Edit button.
Two options should appear: Recipe and Meal.
Choose the desired option.

In the Recipe Editor

The recipe editor has has 5 button options:

  • The New Button
  • The Open Button
  • The Save Button
  • The Save As Button
  • The Close Button
